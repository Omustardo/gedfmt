<a name="Biography"></a><h2> <span class="mw-headline">Biography</span></h2>
<dl><dd>From notes by <a href="/wiki/Meredith-1187" title="Meredith-1187">Margery Meredith</a>, grandniece of Sophia Cragg, in the possession of <a href="/wiki/Meredith-1182" title="Meredith-1182">David Loring</a>
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>Sophia lived with her mother (Harriet) and looked after until she died. Sophia then married Peter Howard, widower of her sister Elizabeth, know as 'Tib'
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>They went to live in Holmpton on the Yorkshire coast where Peter had a pony and trap. Nora (Watson nee Meredith) went to stay with them when she was recovering from a mastoid operation. Mother (Dora Cunliffe Wood), Reg and Margery joined Nora at Holmpton for a holiday. Margery was only about 2 years old and was fed on Peter's special curd pie which no one else would touch.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>Peter died at Holmpton and is buried in St Nicholas churchyard.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>Sophia lived in various places afterwards - Blackpool, Eastbourne,Brighton, St Annes and finally went to live with Harriet Alice and Gertrude, her nieces, in Maidstone Road, Heaton Mersey, Manchester. She died tragically of burns in Stockport Hospital after getting out of bed (against instructions) while Gertrude was out shopping.
</dd></dl>

<a name="Time-line"></a><h2> <span class="mw-headline">Time-line</span></h2>
<a name="1858_Birth"></a><h3> <span class="mw-headline">1858 Birth</span></h3>
<dl><dd>Peter Thompson Howard, son of William Howard and Margaret nÃ©e Preston, was born in 1858<sup id="_ref-0" class="reference"><a href="#_note-0" title="">[1]</a></sup> in Yorkshire and baptised on the 7th November, 1858<sup id="_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#_note-1" title="">[2]</a></sup> at Hull, Yorkshire, England.
</dd></dl>
<a name="1882_Marriage"></a><h3> <span class="mw-headline">1882 Marriage</span></h3>
<dl><dd>On the 7th December, 1882<sup id="_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#_note-2" title="">[3]</a></sup> Peter Thompson Howard (Clerk), son of William Howard (Clerk), married Elizabeth Outhwaite, daughter of James Outhwaite (Joiner), at St Mark, West Gorton, Lancashire, England. The marriage was witnessed by William Outhwaite and Sophia Cragg Outhwaite
</dd></dl>
<a name="1909_Marriage"></a><h3> <span class="mw-headline">1909 Marriage</span></h3>
<dl><dd>On the 17th July, 1909<sup id="_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#_note-3" title="">[4]</a></sup> Peter Thompson Howard, son of William Howard (deceased) and widower of Elizabeth (Howard) Outhwaite, married Sophia Cragg Outhwaite, daughter of James Outhwaite (deceased) and sister of his first wife, at St Clement, Longsight, Lancashire, England.
</dd></dl>
<a name="1923_Death"></a><h3> <span class="mw-headline">1923 Death</span></h3>
<dl><dd>Peter passed away in 1923<sup id="_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#_note-4" title="">[5]</a></sup> at Holmpton, Yorkshire, England and is buried in St Nicholas churchyard.<sup id="_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#_note-5" title="">[6]</a></sup>
</dd></dl>
